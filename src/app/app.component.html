Frequently Asked Questions

How to use Accentify?

Accentify is currently under active development. We're working hard to bring you the best accent conversion experience. Stay tuned for updates. We'll notify you as soon as we launch!

Does Accentify work with...?

We're developing Accentify to work with various online chatting and virtual meeting platforms. Our planned integrations include Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, WhatsApp, Discord, and many more. These integrations are currently in development and will be available upon launch. Accentify will support use cases such as: sales interactions, gaming voice chat, content creation, and pronunciation improvement.

What languages and accents do you support?

We're developing support for the English language initially, with plans to expand to more languages in future updates. For English accents, we're working on support for Indian, Pakistani, Georgian and other accents. The final list of supported languages and accents will be announced closer to launch.

Is it okay to use Accentify even if my English pronunciation is poor?

Absolutely! We believe there is no "poor pronunciation". It's vitally important for users to retain their native accents. The Accentify system we're developing will allow users to speak naturally, and it will modulate accents for clarity while preserving your unique voice characteristics.

Does Accentify only change accents, or can it translate too?

We're developing Accentify to do both! Our planned features include accent neutralization for global clarity as well as real-time translation capabilities. The translation feature will transform your speech into multiple languages instantly, making cross-language meetings effortless. These features are currently in development.

How is Accentify different from regular translation apps?

Unlike text-based translators, Accentify is being developed to operate in real time and focus on spoken communication. It will work directly in your live calls or streams, maintaining natural voice flow without awkward pauses or robotic tones. This real-time processing capability is a key focus of our development efforts.

When will Accentify be available?

Accentify is currently under active development, and we're making great progress. While we don't have an exact launch date yet, we're committed to delivering a high-quality product.
